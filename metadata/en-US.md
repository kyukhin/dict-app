## Description

LibreDict is a lightweight, ultra-fast dictionary built for focus and privacy. With over 300,000 words across five built-in dictionaries, you can search and learn without ever needing an internet connection.

Key features:
- 100% Offline: Every dictionary lives on your device — perfect for travelers and students.
- Five Dictionaries Included: English (WordNet), Russian–English, Spanish–English, English–Spanish, and Arabic–English.
- Four App Languages: Use LibreDict in English, Russian, Spanish, or Arabic — with full right-to-left layout for Arabic.
- Smart Arabic Search: Type Arabic words without diacritics and still find the vocalized entries.
- Color-Coded Results: Each dictionary has its own colored stripe, so you instantly see where a word comes from.
- Your Order, Your Way: Sort by relevance or put your preferred dictionaries first, and drag to reorder them.
- Expandable Library: Import your own custom dictionaries via .json or .sqlite files.
- Privacy First: No ads, no tracking, no accounts, no data collection.
- Minimalist Design: A clean, fast interface optimized for readability.
- Manage Sources: View detailed statistics for each dictionary and toggle any of them on or off.

LibreDict stays out of your way — just you and the words.
