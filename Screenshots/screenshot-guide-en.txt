App Store Screenshot Guide
==========================

Each headline = Primary caption (first line) + Subcaption (second line).
One accent word per headline marked with *asterisks* (render in Accent Blue #2D7FF9).

COLOR PALETTE
-------------
Deep Blue    #1A3A5C   Primary headline text, top bars
Accent Blue  #2D7FF9   Call-to-action accents, highlights
Sky Blue     #E8F1FB   Screenshot background (light)
Soft White   #F7F9FC   Card/content backgrounds
Slate Gray   #5A6978   Secondary text, descriptions


LAYOUT TIPS
-----------
- Sky Blue (#E8F1FB) background
- Device mockup centered
- Primary caption in Deep Blue (#1A3A5C) above or below the device; subcaption smaller, in Slate Gray (#5A6978)
- One accent word per headline in Accent Blue (#2D7FF9)
- Keep it clean and readable at small sizes
